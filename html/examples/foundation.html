Cards

Container component with three variants.

Default Card

Standard card with subtle border and shadow.

This is the default card style with a 1px border and small shadow. It works well for most use cases.

Elevated Card

No border, medium shadow for depth.

The elevated variant removes the border and uses a medium shadow to create a floating effect.

Bordered Card

Thicker border, no shadow.

The bordered variant uses a 2px border with no shadow for a flat, structured look.
